Quick Start

Step 1

Open `/broadcast` on phone

Step 2

Allow camera + mic permission

Step 3

Open `/dashboard` on viewer device

Important for Phone Camera

  • Phone browser pe camera mostly HTTPS par hi kaam karta hai.
  • In-app browser ke bajay Chrome/Safari directly use karein.
  • Agar camera blocked ho to browser settings me permission reset karein.

LAN `http://192.168.x.x` URL par kuch devices camera block karte hain. Reliable demo ke liye HTTPS tunnel (ngrok) use karein.
